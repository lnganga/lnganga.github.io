TRAIL OF LIGHT

TRAIL OF LIGHT

Trail of Light is from a Berlin Sound Design Competition and at the beginning of my fall semester in my sound design and creation course.Tasked with choosing a video from the competition, I meticulously crafted the entire sonic landscape which encompasses the music, the sound effects and any other sonic effect from scratch. In this assignment, I had to use applications like ableton to create the music and adobe audition Leveraging tools like Ableton for music creation and Adobe Audition for precision, this assignment stands as a testament to my proficiency in sound design and my ability to bring visual narratives to life through immersive audio experiences.
DYSTOPIAN DINOSAUR
Dystopian Dinosaur was my first attempt at sound design in my audio production class. In this assignment, we had to record sound for our daily life and incorporate it into the design. I ended up using a baby crying towards the end to amplify the theme of dystopia. I keep this video on my portfolio as a benchmark for where I started and as I continue to grow and improve, this video will be proof of how far I have come.
BAKGAT
G Bakgat is a particpatory documentary that I created in collaboration with Steven Myat, together we were able to tell my story on becoming an amapiano dj. In this project, I was co-producer and director. This was also my first being a lead sound designer on such a project. I had an amazing experience from coming up with the concept to filming. Hopefully, I will be able to add more projects like this to my portfolio.
